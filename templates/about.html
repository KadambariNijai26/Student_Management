Student Management System

This Student Management System is developed using Django framework. The project helps schools and colleges manage student records, attendance, marks, fees details, teacher management, and admin activities.

Students can register and log in to view their:

  • Attendance Records
  • Marks & Results
  • Fees Status
  • Course Information

Teachers and Admins can manage:

  • Student Data
  • Attendance Tracking
  • Marks Entry
  • Fees Management
